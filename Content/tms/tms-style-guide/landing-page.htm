  • CDW: Murtha Baca and Patricia Harpring, eds. Categories for the Description of Works of Art. www.getty.edu/gri/standard/cdwa/.

  • Groves: Jane Turner, ed. Dictionary of Art. 34 vols. New York: Grove, 1996.

  • MWGD: Merriam-Webster’s Geographical Dictionary, 3rd ed. Springfield, Mass.: Merriam-Webster, Incorporated, 2001.
  • Official Museum Directory: The Official Museum Directory. Washington, D.C.: American Association of Museums, published annually. Consult the most recent edition.
  • PES: Department of Publications and Editorial Services
  • TGN: The Getty Research Institute. Getty Thesaurus of Geographic Names. http://www.getty.edu/research/conducting_research/vocabularies/tgn/
  • ULAN: Getty Research Institute. Union List of Artist Names. http://www.getty.edu/research/conducting_research/vocabularies/ulan/.
